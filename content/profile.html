Establishment & Profile

Interested to know about us...then continue reading...

Currently we are manufacturing the entire range required for domestic market.

  1. Depressed centre wheels – Diameter 100mm to 230mm (Resin bonded)
  2. Cut off wheels – Diameter 25mm to 350 mm. (Resin bonded)
  3. Mounted Points
  4. Swing Frames And Plain Wheels
Established in 1992, Super Abrasives specializes in the design, manufacture and application of grinding and cutting products and services for a wide range of industries. These include: automotive, tool & cutter fabrication, Machining, as well as many others. We have our own manufacturing plant based at 9-B, M.I.D.C., Jaysingpur with self-owned equipment and vendor.
The wheels manufactured in our unit can be classified as better quality output. We are fully developed and approved under the strict vigilance of our quality control. Also we are thoroughly tested as per various technical standards in the laboratory.
Marketing and Sales can be termed to be our frontline asset and we are backed up with the best quality product for the same. Being in business for past two decades, we are confident for being compared with big fishes in the business for quality.
We fully understand the scope of a better quality product in highly competitive world of business today. We also feel the necessity to be distinguished and identified as a unique holder of dignified product. We can definitely provide a higher edge over the competitors in technical terms. Considering the niche to remain survived in this market, we look forward to get associated with highly reputed stake-holders in the market.
