Safety Measures

  • After mounting allow the wheel to run freely at its recommended speed for at least 5 min. Apply this test run for both new wheels as well as old wheels, re-mounted for grinding.
  • Operator must compulsorily wear safety goggles & face shields to protect himself from sparks & swarf.
  • Use protective aprons, gloves, safety shoes, mask to enable operator to work safely.
  • Only certified & trained person should mount the wheel.
  • Check wheels for damage before mounting. “Ring test” helps the user to ensure that the wheel is in good condition.
  • Do not use damaged or dropped wheels.
